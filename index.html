The brief

Northwind Traders is a global food & beverage distributor. Sales, Marketing, and Service each kept their own customer data in spreadsheets and email. Our team was asked to propose three transactional and three analytical systems to fix it — and to prototype the most important one. This is that prototype.

Team 3 — Delanie Chuma, Andrew Deal, Alejandro Gutierrez Pineda, Harrison Smith, Landon Tavenner
The flow

Five steps. One record.

01

Customer intake

Sales rep creates one unified profile. No spreadsheet. No retyping.

02

Profile created

The record is linked across Sales, Marketing, and Service in the same instant.

03

Order placed

Every order ties back to the profile — visible to anyone who opens it.

04

Loyalty recalculated

Tier auto-updates from lifetime value. No reconciliation, no quarterly cleanup.

05

Customer 360

Every team sees the same record. The whole point of the system.
