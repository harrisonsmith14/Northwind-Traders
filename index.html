The brief

Northwind Traders is a global food & beverage distributor. Sales, Marketing, and Service each kept their own customer data in spreadsheets and email. Our team was asked to propose three transactional and three analytical systems to fix it — and to prototype the most important one. This is that prototype.

Team 3 — Delanie Chuma, Andrew Deal, Alejandro Gutierrez Pineda, Harrison Smith, Landon Tavenner
